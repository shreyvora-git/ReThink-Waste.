About the Waste Segregation Portal

Our Mission & Vision

The Waste Segregation Portal was created with a singular focus: to be a catalyst for **Sustainable Cities and Communities (SDG 11)**. We envision a future where waste management is seamless, efficient, and fundamentally supports a cleaner planet.

Our mission is to **educate, empower, and enable** every citizen to participate effectively in waste segregation, turning waste from a problem into a valuable resource.

Commitment to SDG 11

We believe that proper waste segregation is the cornerstone of urban sustainability. By ensuring waste is correctly sorted at the source, we directly contribute to:

  • **Reduced Pollution:** Less material ends up in landfills, mitigating soil and water contamination.
  • **Resource Efficiency:** Increased recycling rates, conserving natural resources.
  • **Public Health:** Reduced exposure to hazardous and unsanitary waste conditions.

Our Impact

We are a dedicated team driven by technology and environmental consciousness. This portal serves as a central hub for essential waste information, complaint submission, and awareness materials, providing a transparent and accountable platform for civic action.
