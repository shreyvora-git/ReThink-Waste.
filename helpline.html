Frequently Asked Questions

What do I do with e-waste (electronics)?

Please select your location above and call the "Hazardous Waste" number. Do not mix e-waste with household trash.

My garbage wasn't collected today. What's the fastest way to report it?

The fastest way is to use our Online Complaint Form. You can also call the "General Inquiry" or "Municipal" number for your location.
